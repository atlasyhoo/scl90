Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are the assessments scientifically valid?

Yes, we use established clinical scales (SAS, SDS, SCL-90) and widely-recognized personality models (MBTI, Enneagram, Holland Codes) that have been validated through research and clinical practice.

Can I use this app instead of seeing a therapist?

No. This app is designed for self-awareness and monitoring purposes only. It does not replace professional mental health care. If you're experiencing significant distress, please consult a licensed mental health professional.

How often should I take the assessments?

For mental health scales (SAS, SDS, SCL-90), we recommend taking them every 2-4 weeks to track changes. Personality assessments can be taken less frequently, as personality traits are relatively stable.

Is my data private?

Yes, all your assessment data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?

First, try restarting the app. If the issue persists, clear the app cache or reinstall the app. If problems continue, please contact us at atlas19870918@gmail.com with details about your device and the issue.
