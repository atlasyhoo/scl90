Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026
Core Statement: This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or test data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Welcome to our Mental Health & Personality Test App (the "App"). We believe privacy is a fundamental right. Therefore, this App is designed with a "local-first" architecture to provide you with a safe environment for self-exploration.

1. Data Collection Declaration

We do not collect any data.

  • No User Accounts: The App does not require registration, login, or collection of email addresses, phone numbers, or names.
  • No Test Data Upload: All answers you provide and reports generated during tests (such as SCL-90, MBTI, Anxiety/Depression screenings, Career tests) are stored strictly on your local device. They are never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • No Device Identifiers: We do not collect IDFA, IDFV, IP addresses, or device serial numbers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: This App does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, AppsFlyer).

2. How Data is Stored

All test records, historical results, and preferences are stored in your device's local storage (iOS Sandbox).

  • If you delete the App, all data will be permanently lost as we do not have cloud backups.
  • If you uninstall and reinstall the App, previous test records cannot be recovered.
  • You can manually delete specific test records or clear all data at any time within the App settings.

3. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no sharing, selling, or renting of data to any third parties.

4. Medical Disclaimer

The results provided by this App (including SCL-90, SAS, SDS, etc.) are for entertainment, self-awareness, and reference purposes only. They do not constitute a medical diagnosis or professional psychological advice. If you are experiencing severe psychological distress or an emergency, please consult a qualified healthcare professional or contact local emergency services immediately.

5. Children's Privacy

Since this App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children. Children may use the App under parental guidance without risk of data leakage.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we decide to change our data practices in the future (though we have no current plans to do so), we will update this policy and notify you prominently. However, for the current version and the foreseeable future, this App will maintain its "Zero Data Collection" principle.
